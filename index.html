Baked Fish

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Fish fillets 2
Chilli lime seasoning 1 tbsp

Step 2: Bake in the oven for 15 mins.

Brussel Sprouts

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Brussel Sprouts 1 lb
seasoning 1 tbsp
olive oil 3 tbsp

Step 1: Preheat oven to 425 F.

Step 2: Cut brussel sprouts and mix oil and seasonings.

Step 3: Bake in the oven for 15-20 mins.

Carrot Cake

  • Time: 120 mins
  • Serves: 18
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Vegetable oil 1 1/4 cups
White sugar 2 cups
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp
AP flour 2 cups
Baking Soda 2 tsp
Baking Powder 2 tsp

Step 1: Rub the seasonings.

Step 2: Bake in the oven for 15 mins.
